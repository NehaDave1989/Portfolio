Our Services

Skin care therapy/ Facial Therapy

Skin care therapy refers to a range of treatments and practices aimed at improving the health and appearance of the skin. It can include professional treatments performed by estheticians or dermatologists, as well as at-home skincare routines. The goal is to address various skin concerns, such as acne, aging, dryness, hyperpigmentation, and sensitivity.

Special Effect Makeup

Special effect makeup, often abbreviated as SFX makeup, is a specialized field of makeup artistry used in film, television, theater, and other media to create realistic or fantastical effects that cannot be achieved with traditional makeup techniques. This type of makeup can be used to transform actors into various characters, creatures, or even simulate injuries, aging, and other conditions.

Exclusive Nail Art

Nail art is a creative and intricate way of decorating nails, often involving a variety of techniques and materials to produce unique and eye-catching designs. It has become a popular form of self-expression and a significant aspect of the beauty and fashion industries.

Hair Treatment/Hair Cut/ Hair Color

Hair treatments are specialized procedures or products used to maintain, improve, or repair the health and appearance of hair. They address various hair concerns, including damage, dryness, frizz, thinning, and scalp issues.

Bridal Makeup

Bridal makeup is a specialized form of makeup designed to enhance the bride's natural beauty and ensure she looks her best on her wedding day. It's essential to follow a good skincare routine leading up to the wedding to ensure the skin is in its best condition.
